Privacy Policy

"At Zara Therapy Clinic, your privacy is our priority. We are committed to safeguarding your personal information and ensuring confidentiality in all interactions. Below, we address common questions about how we collect, use, and protect your data to provide a secure and trusted experience."

We collect personal details such as name, contact information, and medical history when you use our services.

We collect information via appointment bookings, online forms, and direct communication with clients.

Your data is used for scheduling appointments, providing therapy services, and improving our support systems.

We implement security measures such as encryption, secure databases, and restricted access to protect your personal data.

You may request to access or update your personal data by contacting us at info@zaratherapy.com.

Security

"Zara Therapy Clinic prioritizes the security and confidentiality of your personal information. We implement high IT security standards to protect our website and services from potential security breaches. Our systems follow a strict Information Security Management System (ISMS) and undergo regular external audits to maintain data protection. However, like any online service, using our website comes with inherent security risks. While we take extensive measures to safeguard your data, no system is entirely immune to security threats. We encourage users to take proactive steps, such as using strong passwords and secure devices, to further protect their information. Your privacy remains our top priority, and we continuously strive to enhance our security measures."

Confidentiality

Zara Therapy Clinic therapists may document session transcripts or clinical notes as part of your file. These records are securely stored and managed in accordance with our Privacy Policy and adhere to the same strict confidentiality and record-keeping standards as traditional face-to-face healthcare services.
To learn more about how we handle and protect your personal information, please refer to our Privacy Policy above.
